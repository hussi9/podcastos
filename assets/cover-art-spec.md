# Desi Daily Podcast Cover Art Specification

## Requirements
- **Size**: 3000x3000 pixels (square)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File size**: Under 512KB for Apple Podcasts

## Design Concept

### Option A: Modern Minimalist
- Background: Deep saffron/orange gradient (#FF6B35 to #FF9F1C)
- Large white text: "DESI DAILY"
- Subtitle: "News for South Asian Americans"
- Microphone icon or sound wave element
- Clean, modern sans-serif font (Poppins or Montserrat)

### Option B: Cultural Fusion
- Background: Navy blue (#1A365D) with subtle mandala pattern
- Gradient accent in saffron/green (Indian flag colors)
- Bold "DESI DAILY" in white
- Tagline: "Your Daily Dose of Desi News"
- Podcast mic icon

### Option C: Tech-Forward
- Dark background with gradient mesh
- Neon-style "DESI DAILY" text
- Waveform visualization graphic
- Modern, techy feel appealing to H1B/tech crowd

## Color Palette
- Primary: #FF6B35 (Saffron Orange)
- Secondary: #138808 (India Green)
- Accent: #1A365D (Navy Blue)
- Text: #FFFFFF (White)

## Tools to Create
1. **Canva** (free): https://www.canva.com/create/podcast-covers/
2. **Adobe Express** (free): https://www.adobe.com/express/create/podcast-cover
3. **AI Generation**: Use DALL-E, Midjourney, or Ideogram
